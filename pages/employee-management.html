Employee Management
  • Maintain detailed employee profiles.
  • Monitor attendance using geo-location and advanced timesheets.
  • Automate payroll processing with integrated work hour tracking.
    • Maintain Employee
  • A well-structured employee profile system helps store essential information, including personal details, job roles, skills, performance records, and certifications.
  • Keeping a centralized database ensures that HR teams can easily access and update records whenever required.
  • These profiles serve as a reference for performance evaluations, promotions, and role-based access to company resources.
    • Monitor attendance
  • Implementing geo-location tracking ensures that employees mark attendance only from authorized work locations, reducing instances of time fraud.
  • Advanced timesheets help track working hours, break times, and overtime, ensuring accurate work-hour calculations.
  • Real-time monitoring allows HR and managers to analyze workforce productivity and make data-driven decisions.
    • Automate payroll
  • Automated payroll systems eliminate manual errors by linking work hour tracking with salary calculations.
  • Integration with attendance records ensures precise salary computation, including overtime pay, deductions, and incentives.
  • Payroll automation reduces administrative workload, ensuring timely salary disbursement and compliance with tax regulations.
