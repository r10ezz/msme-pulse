Payroll management
  • Automated Payroll Generation with Compliance Handling
  • Centralized Salary Record Management
  • Expense Tracking and Comprehensive Tax Reports
    • Payroll Generation
  • The system automatically calculates employee salaries based on work hours, deductions, bonuses, and applicable taxes, ensuring accuracy and compliance with labor laws.
  • Payroll processing includes automated payslip generation, direct deposit integration, and compliance with statutory deductions such as PF, ESI, and professional tax.
  • Employees receive instant notifications about their salary credits, along with digital payslips accessible through a self-service portal.
    • Centralized Salary Record
  • Maintain a secure and centralized database of employee salary records, including pay history, tax deductions, bonuses, and incentives.
  • Salary adjustments, revisions, and arrears are automatically recorded, providing a clear audit trail for financial transparency.
  • Role-based access allows HR teams and finance departments to manage salary details while ensuring data privacy and security.
    • Expense Tracking
  • Employees can submit expense claims with receipts, and the system tracks approvals and reimbursements in real time.
  • The platform generates detailed reports on salary structures, company TDS liabilities, and individual employee TDS deductions for tax filing purposes.
  • Automated tax calculations and integration with government portals help businesses stay compliant while providing employees with necessary tax documents for easy filings.
